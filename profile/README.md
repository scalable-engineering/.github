# Scalable Engineering

*Engineering practices and patterns that hold strong from prototype to planet-wide production.*

What works at ten users must not collapse at ten million. These are the disciplines, guardrails, and battle-tested patterns that keep velocity high and incidents low as the zeros multiply.

### The Disciplines

- ⚙️ Deployment pipelines, progressive rollouts, and automated rollback strategies
- 📈 Observability stacks: structured logging, distributed tracing, and SLO dashboards
- 🧪 Testing patterns from unit to chaos, with contract tests in between
